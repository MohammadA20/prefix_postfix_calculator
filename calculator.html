Explanation: Prefix will calculate from right to left so it will 3 then 2 then check * is operator or operant?
Operator then * is multiplication will multiply 2 by 3, continue with 9 take 9 so we have now
6 and 9 after that we have + it's operator so it will add 9 with 6 to give 15 final result

- Postfix: Operand 1 -> Operand 2 -> Operator like 923*+ calculates 2 * 3 = 6 then 9 + 6 = 15

Explanation: We will begin with 9 take in stack then check 2 is operator? No, take in stack, 3 is operator?
No, take in stack, * is operator? Yes, then take the last 2 numbers in stack which are 2 and 3 and multiply
with each other to give 6. We have in stack 6 and 9, continue with + it's operator so it will
add 9 with 6 to give 15 final result
